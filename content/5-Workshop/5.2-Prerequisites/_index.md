## Objective

Ensure the AWS account is ready to deploy the whole workshop.

## Service explanation

IAM, VPC, S3, CloudTrail, GuardDuty, and CloudWatch are services that will be used throughout this workshop.

## Step 1. Verify IAM

- Search for `IAM`.
- Confirm that the account has the required permissions.


## Step 2. Verify the main services

- Open `VPC`, `EC2`, `S3`, `CloudTrail`, `GuardDuty`, and `CloudWatch`.
- Ensure the consoles open correctly.

## Validation

- The account is ready.
- You can continue with the next steps.

![Validation](/images/5-Workshop/5.1-Workshop-overview/valid-iam.png)
