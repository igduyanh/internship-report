# Reflection Report: "Mini Meetup – First Cloud AI Journey"

## Purpose of the Event

The Mini Meetup was organized to provide students and beginners with an opportunity to explore the latest trends in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence (AI). The event also featured practical insights from industry professionals, covering learning experiences, career development, and the essential skills required to meet the demands of today's technology job market.

## Speakers

- **Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung** – *Job Market & Career Trend*
- **Mr. Vinh Banh** – *Data Engineering Career Journey*
- **Ms. Nhu Tran** – *AI Ready Fresher*
- **Mr. Khang Nguyen** – *Career Development & Growth Mindset*

---

# Key Highlights

## 1. Job Market & Career Trend – Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung

This session provided an overview of the Cloud Computing industry in Vietnam and discussed its future growth prospects.

Key points included:

- An overview of the Cloud Computing market in Vietnam.
- Analysis of AWS's investment and expansion in Vietnam from 2015 to 2026.
- Introduction to companies currently deploying their infrastructure on AWS.
- Discussion of career opportunities such as Cloud Engineer, DevOps Engineer, Solutions Architect, Data Engineer, and Security Engineer.
- Insights into the technical skills and qualifications that employers are currently seeking.

---

## 2. Data Engineering Career Journey – Mr. Vinh Banh

Mr. Vinh Banh shared his professional journey, from studying Data Engineering to working at startups and major technology companies.

The session covered:

- Strategies for building an AWS and Data Engineering learning roadmap.
- Essential foundational knowledge for pursuing a career in data.
- Personal experiences working in startup environments and large tech companies.
- Technical and soft skills necessary for long-term career growth.
- Practical advice for students who are beginning their careers in Data Engineering.

---

## 3. AI Ready Fresher – Ms. Nhu Tran

This presentation focused on how students can prepare themselves for an AI-driven workplace.

Main topics included:

- The essential skills expected from fresh graduates.
- Effective ways to use AI as a learning and productivity tool.
- The importance of critical thinking, self-learning, and strong technical foundations.
- Building a professional profile and continuously improving skills to adapt to rapid AI advancements.

---

## 4. Career Development & Growth Mindset – Mr. Khang Nguyen

Mr. Khang Nguyen emphasized the importance of developing a growth mindset and preparing for long-term career success.

Key takeaways included:

- Always ask "Why?" to understand concepts deeply instead of simply memorizing them.
- Maintain a lifelong learning mindset and continuously improve yourself.
- Participate in real-world projects, hackathons, and community activities to gain practical experience.
- Develop teamwork, communication, and networking skills.
- Focus on learning opportunities and career development rather than only considering the salary of the first job.
- Employers value a positive attitude, willingness to learn, and adaptability more than grades or academic qualifications alone.

I also learned more about the current job market, popular career paths, and the skills employers expect from new graduates. # My Experience at the Event

Participating in the **Mini Meetup – First Cloud AI Journey** gave me the opportunity to learn from professionals working in Cloud Computing, Data Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence. Although I attended the event online, I was able to follow every session and gained a great deal of valuable knowledge.

I was particularly impressed by **Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung's** presentation, which provided a detailed analysis of the rapid growth of the Cloud Computing market in Vietnam and the increasing demand for AWS-related positions. His insights gave me a much clearer understanding of future career opportunities in this field.

The presentation by **Mr. Vinh Banh** offered practical perspectives on the career path of a Data Engineer, from academic preparation to working in startups and large technology companies. His experiences helped me better understand the knowledge and skills required to pursue this profession.

In addition, **Ms. Nhu Tran** shared useful advice on preparing students for the AI era, especially on how to leverage AI effectively for learning and work. Meanwhile, **Mr. Khang Nguyen** emphasized the importance of maintaining a growth mindset, asking meaningful questions, taking initiative, and gaining real-world experience through projects and community involvement.

Overall, the event not only helped me stay informed about the latest trends in Cloud Computing and AI but also provided clearer guidance for my future career. The knowledge and experiences I gained will serve as a valuable foundation as I continue my studies, develop my skills, and prepare for my internship and future career after graduation.
